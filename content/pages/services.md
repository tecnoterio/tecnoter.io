Tecnoter.io delivers enterprise-grade infrastructure solutions for the modern web. We specialize in building scalable, observable, and automated systems that empower teams to ship faster and operate with confidence.

## What We Do

### DevOps Consulting & Implementation
We help teams bridge the gap between development and operations. Our services include CI/CD pipeline design, automation strategies, and cultural transformation to unlock faster delivery cycles.

### Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
We build resilient systems that keep your services online. Our SRE practices include SLI/SLO/SLA definition, incident response automation, and capacity planning to ensure 99.9%+ availability.

### Observability & Monitoring
Visibility is power. We implement comprehensive monitoring solutions using Prometheus, Grafana, and distributed tracing to give you real-time insights into system behavior.

### Infrastructure as Code (IaC)
Reproducible infrastructure is reliable infrastructure. We specialize in Terraform, Ansible, and Pulumi to automate infrastructure provisioning and management.

### Cloud Native Architecture
Kubernetes, containers, and microservices. We help you navigate the cloud-native landscape with architecture design, migration strategies, and operational best practices.

### Security & Compliance
Build secure systems from the ground up. We implement security best practices, compliance frameworks (SOC2, ISO 27001), and automated security scanning into your pipeline.
